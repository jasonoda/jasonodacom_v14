END OF THE GAME WEEZER BROWSER, PIXI.JS
LAUNCH

The End of the Game Game is a project I did for Weezer's song "The End of the Game" in 2020. In the video, Weezer chases around a hipster alien. We came up with the idea of creating an 8-bit game that was really hard to beat. The game pretends like there are prizes to win, but in the end, you just get Rick-Rolled. Works in browser and mobile.

SKRILLEX QUEST SKRILLEX BROWSER

Skrillex Quest is an action RPG, browser game featuring the song "SUMMIT." Dust on the cartridge has corrupted the game. Use your magical sword to fight the glitch and save your world from doom. Battle through a Zelda dungeon, free the Princess ghost, and defeat the evil Skrillex box.

DUST ON THE CHIP --- "These days, if you come across a glitch in your game, there isn't a whole lot you can do about it aside from restarting your console. Back in the days of the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), you couldn't do much about it either, but you could feel like you were doing something about it. I am speaking, of course, of the practice of blowing into your game cartridge and rubbing it on the rug. Nine times out of ten, it would do nothing, but there was that one magical time where you felt like you had actually gone into the game and personally removed the glitch-causing dust particles ruining your game." - Abdullah Saeed, The Creators Project

COWBOY CLUE DUSTIN LYNCH BROWSER, THREE.JS
LAUNCH

The classic board game of clue reimagined cowboy style. Move around the board and avoid enemy pieces and collect clues. The clues are based off of lyrics from Dustin Lynche's song "Who Killed the Cowboy".

FOB TRAIL FALL OUT BOY BROWSER

A parody of Oregon Trail. Pack up your tour van with the essentials (oxen, McNuggets, merch, etc) and follow the tour trail across America. Along the way you'll have to play shows, party with fans, cross dangerous rivers, and hunt for food.

Mageboy is a rogue-like dungeon crawler in the art style of the original Game Boy. Gather gold to upgrade your wand and buy perks. You'll have to levitate your way through an onslaught of bullets being hurled at you by vicious enemies.

PROJECT PERCH BROWSER, THREE.JS
LAUNCH

Perch is a rogue-like game inspired by the art of Monument Valley. The player pigeon's world is being consumed by a black nothingness. Guide our fezzed friend from platform to platform and lead him to victory against the darkness.

PROJECT SLAYGROUNDS BROWSER, PIXI.JS
LAUNCH

Slaygrounds: Night of a Million Critters is a horde survival game with rogue-like elements. Ward off waves of evil animals by collecting gold and upgrading your weapons. Only the strongest will survive the night.
